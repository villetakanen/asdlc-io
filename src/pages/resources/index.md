## Recipes

**[Recipes](/recipes)** — Agent-first how-to guides for specific ASDLC tasks. Copy-paste-ready walkthroughs covering setup, workflows, content authoring, and governance. Written for both LLM execution and human reference.

## Reference Materials

- **[ASDLC Field Manual](/fieldmanual)** — A collated edition of all ASDLC concepts, patterns, and practices in a single file handy for printing or LLM ingestion purposes.
- **[Article Compendium](/resources/compendium)** — Alphabetically-sorted compilation of all ASDLC articles in a single page, optimized for bulk download, scraping, or LLM ingestion (unstructured format).
- **[Legend & Taxonomy](/resources/legend)** — Definitions of statuses, maturities, and content types used in this documentation.
- **[Content Governance Standard](/resources/governance)** — Standards for Frontmatter Schema and Content Governance in the ASDLC repository.
- **[Further Reading](/resources/further-reading)** — A curated feed of thought-leadership articles, external voices, and convergent frameworks aligning with ASDLC principles.

## The Avionics Design System

[The Avionics Design System](/resources/design-system) frames ASDLC as a technical flight manual. By pairing aerospace typography with a tactile, industrial color palette, it establishes a rigorous, timeless foundation for documentation.
