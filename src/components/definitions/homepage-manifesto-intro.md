# Agentic Software Development Lifecycle

For 50 years, software development has been a **Craft**: dependent on individual artisans, manual tooling, and implicit knowledge.

We believe the next era of software engineering is **Industrial**.

ASDLC.io documents the principles, patterns, and standards required to make this transition. The blueprints for the **software factory**—systems where agents serve as the architecture of labor, and humans design, govern, and optimize the flow.
