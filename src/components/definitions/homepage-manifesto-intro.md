# Agentic Software Development Lifecycle

For 50 years, software development has been a **Craft**: dependent on individual artisans, manual tooling, and implicit knowledge.

We believe the next era of software engineering is **Industrial**.

ASDLC.io documents the principles, patterns, and standards for the transition. We document the blueprints for the **software factory**. In these systems, **software agents** – autonomous programs carrying out tasks – form the architecture of labor. Humans design, govern, and optimize the flow.

There is no single right answer to building these systems; thus, **optionality is a feature**, not a bug. 

Patterns in architecture and software engineering gave us a shared, opinionated vocabulary of composable building blocks. ASDLC.io maps the vocabulary of the **Agentic era**.
